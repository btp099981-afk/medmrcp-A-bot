📝 History Taking - MRCP Clinical Approach

1. Patient Introduction:
- Introduce yourself
- Confirm patient's name and age
- Explain the purpose of the consultation
- Obtain consent

2. Presenting Complaint (PC):
- Ask the main reason for attending
- Record symptoms with duration

3. History of Presenting Complaint:

Use SOCRATES for pain:

S - Site
O - Onset
C - Character
R - Radiation
A - Associated symptoms
T - Timing
E - Exacerbating factors
S - Severity

4. Past Medical History:
- Previous illnesses
- Hospital admissions
- Operations
- Chronic diseases

5. Drug History:
- Current medications
- Allergies
- Recent treatments

6. Family History:
- Similar illnesses
- Genetic diseases

7. Social History:
- Smoking
- Alcohol
- Occupation
- Living situation

8. ICE:
Ideas:
What do you think is happening?

Concerns:
Is there anything worrying you?

Expectations:
What were you hoping we could do today?
